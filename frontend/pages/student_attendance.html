🚨 Regulations & Policy

🎓 Minimum Requirement: According to university academic policy, a student must maintain at least 75.0% attendance in each course to be eligible to sit for final semester examinations.

⏰ Late Arrival: Being marked Late counts as present in records, but multiple late arrivals are monitored by faculty and HODs and may affect internal evaluations.

ℹ️ Excused Leaves: For medical emergencies, submit official certificates to the HOD within 3 days of returning to have absences updated as Excused.
